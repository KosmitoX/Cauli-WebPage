Why Choose ClassicSwap?

ClassicSwap is designed with simplicity in mind, not only can we facilitate for your swap, stake your crypto and earn interest on your crypto, safely, securely and openly.


Pure Immutability

The ClassicSwap project being deployed on the ETC network will be completely immutable and under no circumstances will allow a rollback or forks for unnecessary reasons.


Low Fees

Unlike some Decentralized exchanges like UniSwap, PancakeSwap and more, ClassicSwap will have extremely low fees due to Ethereum Classics low network gas fees.


Community Driven

The ClassicSwap project is not incentivized by a treasury, and thus the entire project is driven by passion and community.

Fully Decentralized

ClassicSwap is completely decentralized unlike BSC where it's connected to a centralized exchange, ETC is fully decentralized supported with active PoW miners.
